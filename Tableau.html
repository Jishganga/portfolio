1. BRITISH AIRWAYS REVIEW DASHBOARD

OBJECTIVE:

    objective is to showcase a comprehensive and interactive data visualization dashboard for British Airways reviews, providing insights into various metrics such as overall rating, cabin staff service, entertainment, food, ground service, seat comfort, and value for money. Through this dashboard, users can dynamically explore and analyze reviews based on different criteria like month, traveler type, seat type, aircraft, and continent, empowering them to gain valuable insights into British Airways' performance across different dimensions.

DESCRIPTION:

    Explore the world of British Airways reviews through our interactive dashboard, offering a wealth of insights at your fingertips. With dynamic filters for month, traveler type, seat type, aircraft, and continent, you have the flexibility to tailor your analysis to specific criteria, unlocking nuanced insights into British Airways' performance across different dimensions and regions. Whether you're a data enthusiast, industry professional, or simply curious about British Airways' customer feedback, our dashboard provides a user-friendly platform for exploring and understanding the intricacies of passenger reviews. 2. "COVID 19 INDIA ANALYSIS"

OBJECTIVE:

    Analyze COVID-19 cases in India through data visualization to derive actionable insights and trends. Explore the pandemic's impact on various metrics such as infection rates, testing patterns, and vaccination progress.

DESCRIPTION:

    This Tableau project offers a thorough examination of COVID-19 data in India, drawing from six CSV files and shapefiles to construct interactive visualizations. Dive into the dynamic dashboard, which serves as a window into various aspects of the pandemic's trajectory in the country. Through intuitive visualizations, users can navigate through insights on COVID-19 cases categorized by state, delve into testing details, monitor vaccine administration progress, and explore demographics such as age group distribution and gender analysis. The dashboard's dynamic filtering capabilities empower users to customize their exploration, enabling them to uncover nuanced insights tailored to their specific interests or analytical needs. By providing a holistic view of the COVID-19 situation in India, this project aims to contribute to a deeper understanding of the pandemic's impact, facilitating informed decision-making and strategic planning efforts.
