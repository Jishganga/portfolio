1. FMCG BUSINESS ANALYSIS

OBJECTIVE:

    Analyze FMCG data comprehensively and develop dashboards for regular business performance tracking.

DESCRIPTION:

    I focused on understanding the data, which contained approximately 50,000 records, including details of orders, products purchased, quantities, and sales. After that, I worked on cleaning and preparing the data, creating columns like margin and category using index match functions from a mapping sheet. Additionally, I derived a profit column using sales and margin data, and created quarter and year columns for analysis purposes. Following data preparation, I developed two dashboards: one for sales performance and another for profit analysis. The sales performance dashboard included store-wise and category-wise sales, monthly trends, top-selling products, region-wise sales, and profit analysis. The profit analysis dashboard focused on store-wise and category-wise profit margins, monthly trends, and top-profit products. Both dashboards were made interactive using pivot tables and charts connected to slicers. The goal was to provide valuable insights for informed decision-making.
